# Dog Training

## Basic Commands

Start with sit, stay, and come as foundation commands. Use positive reinforcement with treats and praise immediately after desired behavior. Keep training sessions short, around 5-10 minutes, to maintain the dog's focus and enthusiasm.

## Leash Training

Begin leash training in a low-distraction indoor environment before moving outside. Stop walking when the dog pulls and resume only when the leash is loose. Reward the dog for walking beside you with treats and verbal encouragement.

## Behavioral Issues

Separation anxiety manifests as destructive behavior, excessive barking, or house soiling when left alone. Gradual desensitization involves leaving for short periods and slowly increasing duration. Counter-conditioning pairs the owner's departure with positive experiences like special toys or treats.
