# Homemade Pasta

## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 3 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Pinch of salt

## Making the Dough

Mound the flour on a clean work surface and create a well in the center.
Crack the eggs into the well, add olive oil and salt.
Use a fork to gradually incorporate flour into the egg mixture.
Knead for 10 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic.

## Rolling and Cutting

Divide the dough into four portions. Roll each portion through a pasta
machine, starting at the widest setting and working down to your desired
thickness. Cut into fettuccine, tagliatelle, or your preferred shape.

## Cooking

Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil. Cook fresh pasta
for 2-3 minutes until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta water before
draining. Toss with your favorite sauce, adding pasta water to emulsify.
